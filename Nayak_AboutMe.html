Hello and welcome to my website! My name is Isha Nayak, I am a student at Dublin High School and I am interested in going into the engineering field as a 3D animator. Right now I am taking the elective course Computer Science Principles and I hope to broaden my knowledge and interests in the engineering field by taking courses such as Computer Science Principles and becoming a more hands self learner. I love to draw, work with others, and solve puzzles.
Throughout middle school and in highschool I am continuing to learn that friends and teammates have your back and through good communication and loyalty, projects and assignments can get done properly. Additionally, being creative is crucial to set your assignment out from the others. While I learn a lot through my classes and teammates at school I learn a lot on my own. I have taken Khan Academy courses about what shading artists at Pixar do through the partnership between Khan Academy and Pixar in a Box. I also created many flyers for fundraisers for my Animals and Environment Project Group in a middle school club called the California Scholarship Federation.
In middle school I was a part of a club called the California Junior Scholarship Federation and I was the project manager in charge of the Animals and Environment project group. I played a big part in organizing a popsicle fundraiser to raise money for the Hurricane Harvey victims. We raised over $500 for the victims of the Hurricane Harvey and from that fundraiser. Through the fundraiser I learned about working with others and communicating the dates through flyers and emails. I also learned about planning and predicting to keep the fundraiser in order.
